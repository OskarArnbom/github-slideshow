On-DemanD Culture

is a Stockholm based post punk band. Some of our influences are Joy Division and The Smiths. On-Demand Culture was formed in 2017 and consists of lead vocalists on the guitar, Max Ström and Arvid Häggqvist. One bass player, William Taylor, and a drummer, Oskar Arnbom. Max and Arvid are childhood friends and started making music together at a young age. They later wanted to start their own band and began looking for other components for their projects. They found William through a band finder app and picked up Oskar later on, after hearing about him through a mutual friend.
